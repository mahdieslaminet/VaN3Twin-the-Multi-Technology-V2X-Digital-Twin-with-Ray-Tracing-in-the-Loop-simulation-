# VaN3Twin: Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) Network Simulator

![V2X Communication Model](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/7f/Vehicle-to-everything_%28V2X%29_Communication_Diagram.png)

## Overview
**VaN3Twin** is a comprehensive, high-fidelity simulator for **Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V)** and **Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I)** communications, providing a unified platform for full **V2X network analysis**. The simulator accurately models both **Line-of-Sight (LoS)** and **Non-Line-of-Sight (NLoS)** propagation conditions, incorporating realistic **multipath fading, shadowing, and reflection effects** in complex urban, suburban, and highway scenarios.

By integrating **advanced ray-tracing methodologies** with multi-technology communication models, VaN3Twin enables **end-to-end evaluation of heterogeneous vehicular networks**, including interactions between C-V2X (PC5), 5G NR-Uu, and IEEE 802.11bd technologies. Its architecture supports **scalable scenario generation, reproducible performance assessment, and high-fidelity emulation of dynamic vehicular mobility**, allowing researchers to study network behavior under realistic propagation, mobility, and interference conditions.

Designed as a **research and development platform**, VaN3Twin facilitates the analysis of **safety-critical and non-safety applications, cooperative driving strategies, and next-generation autonomous vehicle communication systems**, bridging the gap between theoretical modeling and practical experimentation in **intelligent transportation systems (ITS)** and **connected, automated mobility ecosystems**.

## Features

- **Comprehensive V2X Scenario Support:** Enables simulation of a wide variety of **Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V)**, **Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I)**, and **Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X)** communication scenarios. Supports protocols including **IEEE 802.11p**, **NR-V2X**, and future multi-RAT vehicular technologies, providing a versatile platform for both academic research and practical evaluation.  

- **High-Fidelity Ray Tracing Engine:** Accurately models **signal propagation**, **reflection**, **diffraction**, and **shadowing effects** in complex urban, suburban, and highway environments. Supports both **Line-of-Sight (LoS)** and **Non-Line-of-Sight (NLoS)** conditions, enabling realistic assessment of channel quality and network performance.  

- **Advanced Performance Metrics Evaluation:** Calculates and monitors key V2X performance indicators, including **Received Signal Strength Indicator (RSSI)**, **Signal-to-Interference-plus-Noise Ratio (SINR)**, **Packet Reception Ratio (PRR)**, **Data Rate (DR)**, **latency**, and **throughput**, providing comprehensive insights into network behavior under diverse conditions.  

- **Integration with Realistic Maps and Traffic:** Seamlessly integrates with **OpenStreetMap (OSM)** for realistic urban and highway topology, and **SUMO** for traffic simulation, enabling **mobility-aware network analysis** and **scenario-based testing** of V2X communication strategies.  

- **Urban and Multi-Path Environment Modeling:** Supports detailed modeling of **multipath fading, NLoS propagation, and shadowing**, capturing complex urban canyon effects, building-induced reflections, and dynamic vehicle-to-vehicle interactions.  

- **Scalable and Extensible Architecture:** Designed to support **city-scale V2X networks**, multiple concurrent scenarios, and multi-technology coexistence (C-V2X PC5, 5G NR-Uu, IEEE 802.11bd). Researchers can extend the simulator to incorporate new communication standards, mobility models, or evaluation metrics.  

- **Simulation-Driven Research Platform:** Provides a **research-oriented, reproducible, and open environment** for exploring emerging V2X applications, autonomous driving communication strategies, cooperative adaptive mobility, and intelligent transportation system (ITS) optimization.  

- **End-to-End Network and System Analysis:** Enables holistic evaluation of **vehicular communication stacks**, from physical-layer channel effects to network-level performance, supporting advanced studies in **beam management, resource allocation, ultra-reliable low-latency communications (URLLC), and AI-driven network optimization**.  

- **Flexible Scenario Design:** Supports dynamic configuration of traffic density, vehicle speed, infrastructure placement, mobility patterns, and environmental conditions, allowing **customized experimentation for diverse research needs**.  

- **Data Export and Visualization:** Generates detailed logs, performance statistics, and simulation traces, enabling **post-processing, visualization, and integration with external data analysis pipelines**.  

- **Collaborative and Open Research Ready:** Structured to support **multi-user studies, collaborative research, and integration with academic projects**, facilitating joint experimentation and high-impact publications.
